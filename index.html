A brief background

Executive producer Scott Harper founded Sunday Night Entertainment in 2001 following his first independent network commission “Cake Night,” a POV behind-the-scenes at an addiction recovery facility. New projects include Clara’s Big Ride, featuring the Canadian Olympian Clara Hughes. Premiering to over a million viewers in January, it’s one part social issue film on the stigma surrounding mental health, one part epic red movie, and one part love letter to Canada.Subsequently Scott wrote, produced and directed Lost Adventures of Childhood — which explored the vanishing phenomenon of unsupervised play among children — continues to sell internationally, 28 countries and counting. It won the Golden Sheaf for Best Science Documentary at the Yorkton Film Festival.
